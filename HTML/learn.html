Picante, Salsa, and Pico are very similar to each other, can you tell them apart?

Salsa

Salsa is typically made of chopped tomatoes, onions, cilantro, and chili peppers, as well as other seasonings such as garlic and lime juice. There are many different types of salsa, ranging in spiciness and texture. Some variations of salsa also include fruits such as mangoes, pineapples, or peaches. Salsa is often served as a dip with tortilla chips, but can also be used as a topping for tacos, burritos, and other Mexican dishes.

Picante

Picante translates to "spicy" in English. Picante contains pureed vegetables to make a smooth sauce.

Pico de Gallo

Pico is a form of salsa made from diced raw veggies. It is normally more mild than the others.
