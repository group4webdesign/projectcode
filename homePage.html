Chuck's Picante

Mild

Cool, casual, flavorful. Perfect for those who enjoy a mild taste with just a hint of heat.

Medium

A delicious blend of fresh tomatoes, onions, and peppers, with a moderate level of heat that adds a kick without overwhelming your taste buds.

Hot

A bold and spicy salsa with a fiery kick that will awaken your taste buds. Made with a blend of fresh and roasted peppers, this salsa is perfect for those who like their food with some heat.

Diablo

For the ultimate heat-seekers, this salsa packs a punch with a blend of the spiciest peppers, garlic, and spices. Not for the faint of heart, this salsa is sure to ignite your taste buds and leave you wanting more (if you can handle it!).
