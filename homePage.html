Fresh Ingredients

Chuck uses only the freshest ingredients to create his picante. Gathered from local producers in the area, Chuck ensures his Picante is made from the best of the best.















Locally Made

Chuck hand produces every jar of his Picante right here in Eau Claire! Locally made means less transportation, less negative environmental impacts, and better taste!
